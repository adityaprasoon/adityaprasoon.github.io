Aditya Prasoon

Software engineer based in Bangalore, India

I've been conjuring up code since my teenage years. It was pure magic then, and honestly, it's still pure magic now that I get paid to write software.

When I first encountered computers, they were still a rarity. The standard display was still that boxy CRT and IBM was still a thing in consumer hardware. Mice used to have a ball and later when the internet reached our homes, connecting required musical squeaks and whistles of a dial-up modem over a phone line.

I graduated in 2014 with a degree in Electronics and Communication Engineering and since then have been working as a Software Engineer in various tech stacks.

I live in Bangalore with my family and our cat. When I step away from the keyboard, I'm usually off on a road trip, lost in a good non-fiction book or tending to my plants.
