Several meeting time planners already exist, inform of mobile apps and well as web apps. However, I am hard-pressed
to find an app which has a similar UI in phone large screens as well as smaller screens. 

The purpose of Globe is to create a simple and intuitive meeting time planner which can be used in devices of all 
screen sizes with equal ease. Additionally, in the spirit of keeping things simple - I plan to have no login option.
All the user preferences shall be stored as local cookies and not stored in server.
